HomeSphere web service is a platform that allows users to remotely control and monitor various devices within their home through an internet connection. These devices may include lighting, thermostats, security cameras, door locks, and home appliances. By integrating with mobile apps, voice assistants, and web interfaces, users can automate routines, receive real-time alerts, and ensure their home is energy-efficient and secure. The system leverages IoT (Internet of Things) technology to enable seamless communication between devices, offering convenience, safety, and improved quality of life.
